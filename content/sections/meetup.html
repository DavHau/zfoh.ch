Zurich Friends of Haskell - General Assembly

Wednesday 10 March, 19:00

Every year the Zurich Friends of Haskell meet for a general assembly as per the statutes of the association: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-8eHAK40hiH5jv79AscWtf3pyUNxx4Ls1LwkpLYK--8/edit?usp=sharing

The general assembly gives you the opportunity to learn what the Zurich Friends of Haskell Association has been doing in this unusual year 2020 and to contribute and help the Zurich Haskell community grow.
Interested? The general assembly is open to all members of the ZFoH. If you are not a member yet, please head over to https://zfoh.ch and sign up for membership.

The agenda is as follows:

1. The board will present a summary of the association's activities in 2020.
2. We will discuss the goals and aspirations of the association for 2021.
3. We will discuss the mode of operation of ZuriHac 2021.
4. The treasurer will present the financial report for 2021.
5. We vote to discharge the board for their work in 2021.
6. We vote on new members of the board.

Note, members of the ZFoH will receive a separate invitation via email. Please RSVP to this meetup event anyway so we get an accurate picture of who will be attending. Thank you!

Looking forward to seeing you at the GA!
